== Description ==

This plugin enables you to hide your pages by removing them from WordPress menus and searches.

It will also add a meta tag to your hidden pages telling the search engines not to index or archive them.

This plugin is ideal for download pages, thank you pages or any other page you want to hide.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the download file if necessary and upload the `pc-hide-pages` folder and all its contents to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in your WordPress admin.
3. Click on the 'Hide Pages' menu under the Settings menu in your WordPress admin.
4. Tick the pages you want to hide from your visitors and the search engines.
5. Click the Save Changes button.
